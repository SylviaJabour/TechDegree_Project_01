About

I’ve been working as an architect in Brazil since 2010. In 2015, my husband got a job in Montreal and we’ve been living here since then.

When I was to come to Montreal, I looked into validating my diploma and I found out that it would take almost the same time a degree in architecture here would. I spent two years studying Archiviz for Architecture, but wasn't easy to get a job in that area in the city where I live, so I took a important decision that was to study to became a web developer. Since then, I've been going throught a lot doubts about my career.

Then, I thought with my self: "it will be better if I start from the scratch in an area that I know have a lot opportunities than insist in something that can take more time to get some professional oportunity."

That's why I'm doing the Tree House course.
